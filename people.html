People & Power

Tech infrastructure doesn't build itself. These are the specific individuals whose decisions, ideologies, and movements shape how US and Chinese tech power operates across Southeast Asia. Click any person to explore their connections to Operators, Domains, and Countries across this site.

Architects Category 1

People who designed the ideological or structural frameworks that govern how tech power operates. Their intellectual and institutional work creates the conditions within which everything else happens.

Operators of Influence Category 2

Executives and founders whose companies are the primary instruments of tech power. They run the machines. Their individual decisions — on export compliance, political alignment, corporate structure — have direct consequences for ASEAN.

Political Enablers Category 3

Political leaders who have made decisions — through strategic choice, pragmatic constraint, or structural capture — that have opened their countries to deep tech penetration by one or both blocs. Alignment assessments are analytical judgements based on documented policy decisions. They change over time.
